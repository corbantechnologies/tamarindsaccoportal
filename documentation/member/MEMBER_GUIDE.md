# Tamarind SACCO: Member’s User Guide

Welcome to the Tamarind SACCO Member Portal! Manage your financial growth with ease.

---

## 📈 Loans & Credit

### The Loan Application Journey
1.  **Select Product:** Choose your loan type.
2.  **Calculate & Apply:** Preview your interest and schedule.
3.  **Amendment Phase:** Respond to any Admin changes by **Accepting** or **Cancelling**.
4.  **Guarantors:** Invite others to guarantee your loan if needed.
5.  **Final Submission:** Submit once fully guaranteed.

### Repayments & Tracking
*   **Balance Updates:** Payments are reconciled and balances are updated in your portal by the **end of the day**.
*   **Schedule:** View upcoming payments and download statements anytime.
