# Tamarind SACCO: Administrator’s Migration Guide 🚀

Welcome to Tamarind SACCO! Follow this 5-phase guide to migrate your institution to our platform.

---

## 🏗 Phase 1: Foundation (System Rules)
Define your GL, Payment Accounts, Fee Types, and Products under the **Setup** menu.

## 👥 Phase 2: Member Migration
Import members via Single Invite, Bulk Form (up to 15), or CSV mass upload.

## 💰 Phase 3: Savings & Fees Migration
Migrate existing balances for savings and fees to ensure a seamless member portal experience.

## 🏦 Phase 4: Loan Migration & Lifecycle
Migrate active loans and manage the ongoing loan application loop.

## 📊 Phase 5: Reports & Validation
Verify the migration using our comprehensive Trial Balance and Portfolio reports.

---

> [!TIP]
> Ensure all your **Loan Products** have their GL accounts correctly mapped before starting Phase 4.
